History

We are the engineers of chaos. Founded in 2013 with lay to the claim of being one of the first starcraft clans ever made when they were made possible late 2013. The small community quickly grew to span over 5 clans all with their own seperate leader and leadership style but all are connected to the original Engineers of chaos. We are a good quality gaming community and offer a variety of events.
